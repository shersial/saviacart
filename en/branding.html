Branding

A trade mark is the hand that holds a company’s reputation.

Stand and see the tools and things around you, the car you ride, the toothpaste you use, the shoes you wear, and even the water you drink all share in carrying brands that stick to our lives and wrap them on every side. After searching for a translation of the word "brand" in the world of marketing, the result was "trade mark " or "commercial identity", but we believe that this translation has some shortcomings. We believe in "SAVIA CART" because we are specialists in the science of marketing that a "trade mark" or "commercial identity" is the name, characteristic, symbol, design, or the sum of all these things that aim to define a specific good or service in order to distinguish it from other competitors.

In our Emirati society and a number of societies in the Arab region, the general public knows the trade mark by the word “brand” and here they mean that it is a well-known and reliable trade mark. Some people may mistakenly believe that the trade mark is the logo, designing a good logo without taking into account all the components of the trade mark is to put lipstick on a "gorilla" as mocked by one of the managers of the "Lander" International Marketing Company. To bring the picture closer, we will ask a simple and logical question, why do we buy certain products and not others Most of the time it is not the price that determines this choice, but rather the value of the product to the consumer. It is not about how much money you have, but what this product means to you and what is it's importance to you personally, and about how much money and alternatives you can give up in order to acquire it. You may find a poet whose income is low, buying a Parker pen for about a hundred dollars, and at the same time you will find an accountant in a global company who uses an ordinary pen worth half a dollar. The salary of the accountant here is much more than that of the poet, but the pen does not mean to him anything other than the implementation of the task of writing financial transactions, but there is an intimate relationship between the poet and the pen that makes him give up a hundred dollars that means much more to him than it does to the accountant. Therefore, the poet will seek to acquire a pen with a well-known reputation, distinctive performance, and guaranteed as a Parker pen. That is why we at "SAVIA CART" devote all our skills and capabilities to the manufacture of your distinctive brand. Trade marks have hidden magic that organizes the relationship between the company and the customer. It can't be built overnight, rather it's the result of a lot of planning, management and marketing.
