Application design and programming

We do not work randomly, but we work according to carefully studied strategies.

Do you have an app idea that you would like to implement on IOS "SAVIA CART" can help you develop your idea into a real application. We will help you to face your competitors strongly and efficiently, and we can also convert your idea to an application that works on the Android system.

Starting from planning, design and implementation, we guarantee you an application that can compete in the Google Android Market App Store.

Excellent results are the result of a disciplined work process

1The analysis and planning stage

Plan the mobile app in a professional way from defining the main objectives of the application and it's business model. The target group is determined from this application, does it need such an application, and what is the added value that the target sample will benefit from. And do an extensive search for:

1- Applications with the same idea as your (competitors).

2- Technical and technical requirements for building the application.

3- Future financing, marketing and application development process.

4- How to make money if the goal is for profit, and this is for example but not limited to.



2 In the design and development stage :

Design a mobile application on Photoshop and start drawing the main interfaces of the application, and development of the prototype of the application and its internal components. In such a way, the main interfaces are defined and the special pathways such as data flow and technical and technical needs. The stage of developing the basic model for the application comes, so that it will be presented to specialists and interested parties. The aim of this stage is to first embody the concept of implementation, before the implementation process begins.



3 Program implementation and testing :

At this stage, the actual construction of the application or the implementation stage is started, and all plans and points that were developed and suggested in the previous steps are implemented, From designing interfaces, building connections, internal screens and other technical needs. One of the most important steps at this stage is to have an account on the platform on which the application will be installed, for example, Google Play, where it may take a period of time, as well as there are fees that must be paid to allow the application to be uploaded and displayed on the target platform. And upon reaching the testing phase of the application in it's initial (beta) version, Where it is possible at this stage to avoid any errors or shortcomings in the previous stages, as well as his experience on various devices, as well as his participation with specialists and decision-makers such as financiers. Also in this stage, an overview of the application is made, focusing on the process of careful review and reviewing each process separately, and then experimenting with the application.



4 And finally- marketing :

After going through these stages and obtaining approval to upload the application in its final version from various application platforms such as Google Play and Apple Store, the marketing phase, which has been clearly prepared in the early stages of building the application, comes and has been agreed upon.
